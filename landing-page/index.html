About Us
Read More
2Xcell is an A.I driven self-paced learning and online education platform that helps a student become a ‘Super Learner’ using Blended Learning Model. Students get “Exclusive Personalized Learning Experience” where they are guided by “Dedicated Mentor” which strengthens the learning base of students and helps them learn in online courses through customized interactive 3D-2D videos at a much faster pace. Inquiry-Based Learning (IBL) helps a student assess himself and measure the learning outcome / Learning index or Lx at subtopic level and provides remarkable insights to help mentor plan the right pedagogy for the student and design a personalized ‘Goal Oriented’ learning plan. Seamless integration of concepts across grades under the “Multi-Grade Program” helps students strengthen the base for the upcoming Grades and explore beyond the curriculum.
